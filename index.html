About the Laboratory

The AI Research Laboratory at ESI focuses on developing intelligent systems that improve education, understand human emotions from data, and enhance healthcare decision-making. Our work combines Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Data Analytics to build impactful real-world solutions.

Research Areas

E-Learning Intelligence

Adaptive platforms, intelligent tutoring, learning analytics, and student modeling.

Sentiment Analytics

NLP, opinion mining, emotion detection, and social media analysis.

Healthcare AI

Medical prediction, diagnostic support, and patient data intelligence.
