About the Laboratory

The Laboratory Team (LabTEL) is a research team within the LMCS Laboratory dedicated to the study and development of advanced e-learning technologies. Its research focuses on learning visualization, artificial intelligence, intelligent systems, and sentiment analytics, with the objective of enhancing the effectiveness and quality of digital learning environments.

Research Areas

E-Learning Intelligence

Adaptive platforms, intelligent tutoring, learning analytics, and student modeling.

Sentiment Analytics

NLP, opinion mining, emotion detection, and social media analysis.

Healthcare AI

Medical prediction, diagnostic support, and patient data intelligence.
